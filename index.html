Tribute to ABDUL HAMID

Abdul Hamid was a renowned military leader and the President of Bangladesh. He was born on January 1, 1944, in Kamalpur, Mymensingh, Bangladesh.

Abdul Hamid dedicated his life to the service of his country. He served in the Bangladesh Army and played a crucial role during the Liberation War of Bangladesh in 1971. He showed exceptional courage and bravery in the face of the enemy and was awarded the Bir Sreshtho, the highest military award in Bangladesh, posthumously.

Abdul Hamid was known for his humility, leadership, and love for his people. He worked tirelessly to improve the lives of the citizens of Bangladesh and was deeply respected and loved by the people.

One of his famous quotes is: "I do not belong to any political party. My only allegiance is to the people of Bangladesh." This quote reflects his dedication to serving the nation and his commitment to the welfare of the people.

"The love of the people inspires me, and their support gives me strength."

Abdul Hamid's legacy as a selfless leader and a true patriot will always be remembered. He passed away on April 20, 2021, but his contributions to Bangladesh will continue to inspire generations to come.
